Semi-Supervised Label Propagation via Entropy-Gated Mixup for Low-Resource Domains

8.4

We propose a semi-supervised framework combining entropy-gated sample selection with domain-adaptive mixup augmentation. The approach addresses catastrophic label noise in low-resource settings by dynamically weighting pseudo-labels based on prediction confidence calibration, achieving significant improvements on benchmarks with fewer than 100 labeled samples per class.
