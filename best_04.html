Reviewer Notes
The proposed type-II band alignment between CsPbBr3 QDs and TiO2 nanotubes is well-supported by recent DFT calculations. The 85% selectivity claim requires careful justification -- current benchmarks for similar systems reach ~70%. Recommending additional control experiments in the plan phase.
