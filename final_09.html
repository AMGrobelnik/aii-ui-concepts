Current Plan
Method: Gradient-Gated Cross-Attention (GGCA) — inserts a lightweight sigmoid gate between the backward pass of cross-attention layers. The gate input is the concatenation of per-modality confidence scores (softmax entropy) and a learned projection of the attention output. During training, the gate learns to suppress gradient updates that would cause attention collapse, while allowing useful cross-modal signal through.
