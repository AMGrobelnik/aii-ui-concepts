Title
Cross-lingual Contrastive Pre-training with Synthetic Code-Switching for Low-Resource Transfer
Abstract
We present a contrastive pre-training framework that generates synthetic code-switched corpora to align multilingual representations across 8 typologically diverse language families. Our method combines language-family-aware curriculum learning with a novel alignment objective that provably converges under mild assumptions. We evaluate on NER, POS tagging, and dependency parsing across 24 low-resource languages, demonstrating consistent gains over XLM-R and mBERT baselines.
